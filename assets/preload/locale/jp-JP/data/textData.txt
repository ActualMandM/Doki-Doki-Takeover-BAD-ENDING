descAddOffset::Adds value to global offset, per song.
descKeyBindings::Change the keybindings of each note.
descDownscroll::Change the direction of upcoming notes.
descGhostTap::Ghost Tapping is when you tap a direction and it doesn't give you a miss.
descJudgement::Customize the game's safe frames. (Left or Right)
descCurJudgement::Current Safe Frames
descFPSCap::Set the game's FPS cap. (Left for -10, Right for +10. Shift to go faster)
descCurFPSCap::Current FPS Cap
descRefreshRate::Refresh Rate
descScroll::Change your scroll speed. (Left for -0.1, Right for +0.1. 1 will be chart default.)
descCurScroll::Current Scroll Speed
descAccuracyMode::Change how accuracy is calculated. (Accurate = Simple, Complex = Millisecond Based)
descReset::Toggle pressing R to game over.
descCustomize::Customize parts of the gameplay HUD to your heart's content.
descCustomizeState::Drag around the score to customize its position. R to reset. Q/E to zoom. Space to pause cam zooming. C to generate combo. Escape to exit.
descDistract::Toggle stage distractions that can hinder your gameplay.
descFPSRainbow::Make the FPS counter display as a rainbow.
descAccuracy::Display accuracy information.
descNPS::Shows your current notes per second.
descPosition::Show the songs current position. (as a bar)
descFPSCount::Toggle the FPS counter.
descReplay::View your previous gameplay.
descFlashing::Toggle flashing lights that can cause epileptic seizures and strain.
descWatermark::Show watermarks that come from Kade Engine.
descBotplay::Showcase your charts and mods with autoplay.
descGFCountdown::Have Girlfriend count down before the song starts.
descKeyBindKBCurrent::Current Mode: Keyboard.
descKeyBindGPCurrent::Current Mode: Gamepad.
descKeyBindTAB::Press Tab to switch.
descKeyBindKBControls::Escape to save, Backspace to leave without saving. Enter to change a keybind.
descKeyBindGPControls::Right Trigger to save, Left Trigger to leave without saving. Start to change a keybind.
descKeyBindBlacklist::is blacklisted!
descCharacterCache::Reduce load times by caching every character on boot. (MAJOR MEMORY USAGE)
descSongCache::Reduce load times by caching every song on boot. (MAJOR MEMORY USAGE)
descMusicCache::Reduce load times by caching every music on boot. (MAJOR MEMORY USAGE)
descSoundCache::Reduce load times by caching every sound on boot. (MAJOR MEMORY USAGE)
descCaching::Reduce load times after boot by loading the selected options above.
descDisableCaching::(press D to disable next boot)
descMiddleScroll::Put your lane in the center or on the right.
descLaneUnderway::Toggles lane underlay.
descLaneUnderwayControl::Current Lane Underlay transparency
descSaveReset::Reset your save file to its defaults.
descDialogueSkip::Press ESCAPE to skip the dialogue.

descHitSound::Play a sound whenever you press a note.
descHitSoundControl::Current Hit Sound volume
descNoteSplash::Adds a splash to any sick notes that you hit!
descMillisecond::Shows the timing of the note that you hit in milliseconds.
